19th September - Dinner with Olympian Gold Medalist Rebecca Adlington

If you weren’t able to drop your laptop and get on your bike to join the TechBikers ride, you can support Tech Bikers by joining our charity dinner with Rebecca Adlington.

During a stellar career, Rebecca won multiple Olympic, World, European and Commonwealth Games gold medals. She competed in four Olympic finals, winning two Gold and two Bronze medals in Beijing and London respectively. She remains the current world record holder in 800m Freestyle, and is undoubtedly the greatest swimmer that Great Britain has ever produced, not to mention one of the most successful female athletes in British Olympic history.

Enjoy an intimate and memorable fine dining experience with Great Britain's most successful ever swimmer. The dinner (3 courses, including wine) will take place on 19th September, 7pm at the Eight Club Moorgate's private dining room overlooking the City of London.

Grab one of the five early bird seats for £150 for the price increases. Sign up here.

Thank you for your support and hope to meet you at dinner.
